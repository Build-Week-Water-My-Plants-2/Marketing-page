Planet Planners

Our Mission

Planet Planners is the innovation planets need to continuously flourish. It is our mission to not only help humans keep their floral pets alive, but to thrive. We provide planet owners with visual and audio reminders of when they need to water their planets. Additionally, Planet planners will tell the owner exactly how much water their planets needs based on the type of planet. It is our goal through this technology that planets will no longer die because of human error.
